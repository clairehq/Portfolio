Project Description

This project is for an interactive design methods course. It is about observing and analyzing the interaction within a participant group and then design a practical solution to solve the biggest problem founded. The purpose of this project is about learning concepts of design practice and related design methods for interaction designers.

Participant Group

Our participant group is MLGB-Esport Club which is an internet café.

Research Process

investigation & ethnography data

I did some interviews with the staff and customers in the café in order to find some potential problems in the cafe and I also investigated the gaming environment in the café to see if there are problems exist. Then, I provided a thematic analysis based on the data we found.

user journey maps

For a clear understanding of staff’s workflow, I examined their’ workflow through particular aspects of their tasks and visualized the activities and interactions performed with positive and negative feelings. Then I present all the information in a clear and organized visual poster.

cultural probes, personas & participatory workshop

We made a game controller box that contains four different probes to explore different opinions from different customers. Two probes are open-ended questions and two probes are closed-ended questions. Then, we created three personas based on the data we have collected through the research. Finally, we designed several activities to focus down on specific areas of our design focus.

Solution

Through the research, the biggest problem I found is most customers are not satisfied with their serving service. The servers in the café have to take reservations, check in/check out computers and serving food/drinks to the customers. During rush hours, they can’t keep fast serving speed and that makes customers not satisfied. Therefore, I come up an idea of an all-in-one app, Taco, which propose to realize customer self-service. Taco is an all-in-one service application associated with MLGB E-sports Club membership system which proposes to realize customer self-service. By using Taco, MLGB E-sports Club’s customers will be guided through its personalized content and interact with MLGB E-sports Club with just one simple touch on their phone. Also, make ease of interaction between players when playing team-based games.

Reflection

Through this project, I learned different research methods during the project and how to design appropriate questionnaire survey based on users type. For example, the customers in the E-sport Club are busy with playing games; therefore we designed our culture probes and participatory workshops easy and simple so that customers can complete them in a quick but interesting way.
