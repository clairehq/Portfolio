Work About

Movie Hat

  • Date: October—November, 2018

  • My Role: User researcher, UI+UX designer

  • Group Members: Hanqiao Li, Ivy Zhao, Pavel Orsher

  • Tools: Sketch, Supernova

Project Description

This project is for an interface design course. It is about researching a domain of group and then designing a complex, interactive user interface that is elegant, detailed, and meets the goals of both client and user. The purpose of this project is about practicing skills such as researching, proposing, prototyping and designing.

Domain of Group

Our domain of group is young people in their 20s.

Research Process

I did some research online focusing on what kinds of activities young people usually do, and what kinds of conflicts they might encounter with. After the research, each of us came up with one idea.

App Concept

We discussed each idea and decided to design an interactive system that helps young people to decide what movie to watch at home based on their preferences. We realized it’s hard and time consuming for many young people to decide what movie to watch on their date or home party. Through this app, they can save time on deciding the movie and find a movie that everyone is interested in.

Persona

I created a persona based on our design focus.

Prototype / Solution

Movie Hat is a movie picker app. Users create their profiles with different preferences. Depends on whom they watch with and their mood, they choose certain movie preferences. Then, users interact with the app by shaking the smartphones to start the movie suggestion process. The app makes it easier for couples or group of friends to decide what they gonna watch together.

Reflection

This project allowed me to practice several design methods such as designing user-centered interfaces with nice user flow and usability testing methods. I learned how to use heuristic evaluation when doing user testing. I also learned how to think like users and make user-centered interfaces.
